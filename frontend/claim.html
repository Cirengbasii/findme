Claim Process

  1. Submit your claim with detailed information and proof of ownership
  2. Verification - Our team will review your claim and supporting documents
  3. Matching - We'll compare your claim with found item reports
  4. Contact - If a match is found, we'll connect you with the finder
  5. Collection - Arrange to collect your item from the finder

Tips for Successful Claims

  • Provide as much detail as possible about the item
  • Upload clear photos of receipts or proof of purchase
  • Describe unique features that only you would know
  • Be honest and accurate in your descriptions
  • Respond promptly to verification requests
